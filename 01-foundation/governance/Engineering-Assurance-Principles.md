# Engineering Assurance Principles

## Purpose
Ensures systems are reliable, auditable, and secure by design.

## Scope
Applies to all engineering domains, projects, and artifacts governed by the EOS.

## Core Principles
1. Technology Agnostic: Applies universally regardless of tech stack.
2. Capability First: Prioritizes business capabilities over technical features.
3. Quality Built-In: Ensures reliability and security at every layer.

## Rules
- Must be adhered to by all engineering personnel.
- Exceptions require an approved Architecture Decision Record (ADR).

## Relationships
Integrates with the overarching EOS Constitution and Governance frameworks.

## Dependencies
None.

## Governance
Owned and maintained by the Chief Engineering Architect.
