# Review Model

## Purpose
To establish the formal evaluation mechanisms required to transition objects through their states and lifecycles.

## Review Types

1. **Architecture Review**
   - *Focus:* Alignment with patterns, constraints, and non-functional requirements.
   - *Authority:* Chief Architect.
2. **Capability Review**
   - *Focus:* Business value, definition completeness.
   - *Authority:* Product Owner.
3. **Security Review**
   - *Focus:* Threat models and risk mitigation.
   - *Authority:* Assurance / Security Lead.
4. **Quality Review**
   - *Focus:* Test coverage and verification evidence.
   - *Authority:* Quality Engineering.
5. **Production Readiness Review (PRR)**
   - *Focus:* Operability, observability, and rollback plans.
   - *Authority:* Operations / Release Engineering.
6. **Repository Review**
   - *Focus:* Adherence to domain boundaries and EOS structure standards.
   - *Authority:* Governance.
7. **Engineering Review**
   - *Focus:* General peer evaluation for code or document hygiene.
   - *Authority:* Peer Engineers.
